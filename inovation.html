About Us

LD College of Engineering fosters a vibrant ecosystem for innovation and entrepreneurship. We provide our students, faculty, and staff with the resources, mentorship, and support to translate their ideas into successful startups. Our innovation and startup cell organizes various workshops, bootcamps, and incubation programs to help budding entrepreneurs develop their business acumen and bring their ventures to life.

Our Initiatives

  • Ideation workshops and hackathons
  • Mentorship programs with industry experts
  • Pre-incubation and incubation support
  • Seed funding opportunities
  • Entrepreneurship development workshops
  • Industry connect and networking events

Be a Part of the Innovation Revolution

Are you an aspiring entrepreneur with a groundbreaking idea? We can help you turn it into reality. Get in touch with our innovation and startup cell to learn more about our programs and resources.
