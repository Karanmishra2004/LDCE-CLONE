About Us

The Placement Cell at LDCE plays a pivotal role in bridging the gap between students and potential employers. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive training and support services to prepare students for successful placements. Our team of experienced placement officers works diligently to connect students with leading companies across various industries.

Placement Process

  • Training & Development Programs (Resume Writing, Interview Skills, Aptitude Tests, etc.)
  • Pre-Placement Talks & Company Interactions
  • Online Placement Portal for Registration & Application Management
  • Conduct of Campus Placements & Online Recruitment Drives
  • Post-Placement Support & Guidance
