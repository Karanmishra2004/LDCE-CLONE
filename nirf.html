What is NIRF?

The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) is a methodology adopted by the Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India, to rank universities and colleges in India. The ranking is based on various parameters such as Teaching, Learning & Resources (TLR), Research and Professional Practice (R&PP), Graduation Outcomes (GO), Outreach, Perception (OP), etc. A high NIRF ranking signifies an institution's commitment to excellence in higher education.
