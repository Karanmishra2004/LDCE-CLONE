About Us

The Department of Electronics at LD College of Engineering is a premier center for education and research in the field of electronics engineering. We offer a rigorous curriculum that provides students with a strong foundation in core electronics concepts, as well as the opportunity to specialize in emerging areas such as VLSI design, embedded systems, communication engineering, and more. Our faculty members are highly qualified and experienced, and they are committed to providing students with a supportive and challenging learning environment.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Engineering (Electronics Engineering)
  • Master of Engineering (Electronics Engineering)
  • PhD in Engineering

Faculty & Research

Our department boasts a team of dedicated and accomplished faculty members who are actively engaged in cutting-edge research. Their areas of expertise include:

  • VLSI Design
  • Embedded Systems
  • Communication Engineering
  • Signal Processing
  • Control Systems

Students have the opportunity to collaborate with faculty members on research projects, gaining valuable experience in the field.

Facilities

The Department of Electronics is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories that provide students with the resources they need to excel in their studies. Our labs include:

  • VLSI Design Lab
  • Embedded Systems Lab
  • Communication Engineering Lab
  • Analog Electronics Lab
  • Digital Electronics Lab
